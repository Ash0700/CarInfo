Lamborghini Huracán

Engine: 5.2-Liter V10

Power/Torque: 610hp and 560Nm (Huracan EVO); 640hp and 600Nm (Huracan STO)

Drivetrain: All-wheel drive (EVO); rear-wheel drive (STO), 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission

0–60 mph: ~ 2.9 seconds (EVO); ~ 3.0 seconds (STO)

Top speed: ~ 202 mph (EVO); ~ 199 mph (STO)

Layout: 2-door, 2-seater, mid-engine

Suspension: Double wishbone front and rear; magnetorheological dampers; available carbon-ceramic brakes
